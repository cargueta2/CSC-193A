Favorite Thriller/Horror Movies to Wacth for Halloween

Every October, I like to watch one or two horror movies on the weekends. Some of the movies I like to watch have some sci-fi, thriller or supernatural aspects to them so I watch a mix of those leading up to Halloween. On the weekend before Halloween, my sisters and I order pizza and grab some snacks and have a movie marathon. We watch some of the classics like Evil Dead, Nightmare on Elm Street, the Scream series and some of our own favorites. Some of my favorites are: the Descent, REC, 28 days laterand some other ones, but my absolute favorite is Grave Encounters.
